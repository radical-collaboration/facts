=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 24-Jan-2011 :
Almeria 2 is a new station and is part of the newly established REDMAR network.
Gauge is a MIROS radar sensor.  Benchmark is MAREOG 3.990m relative to REDMAR datum
3.760m relative to IGN. Station made RLR. RLR year 2006 factor 6.710m.
Datum changed by 13.2cm Oct.2011. Supplied data has been adjusted whole dataset re-loaded.
See paper by B.Perez et al 'Overlapping sea level time series measured using different
technologies: an example from the REDMAR Spanish network'.
