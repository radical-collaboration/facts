=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 24-Jan-2011 :
Palma de Mallorca is a new station first data 2009 and forms part of the newly established REDMAR network.
Gauge is a MIROS radar sensor Benchmark MAREOG-PALMA is 1.627m relative to the
REDMAR datum and 1.790m relative to IGN. See paper B. Perez et al 'Overlapping sea level
time series measured using different technologies: an example from the REDMAR Spanish network'.
