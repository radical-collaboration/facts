=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 24-Jan-2011 :
Mahon is on the island of Minorca and is a new station first data 2009 and
forms part of the newly established REDMAR network. Gauge is a MIROS radar sensor.
Benchmark NGAB MAREOG-MAO is 1.219m relative to the REDMAR datum and 1.336m
relative to IGN. See paper B. Perez et al 'Overlapping sea level time series
measured using different technologies: an example from the REDMAR Spanish network'.
