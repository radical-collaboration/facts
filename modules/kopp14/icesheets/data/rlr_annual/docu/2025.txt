=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 13-Jul-2010 :   
Bugrino is a new station to PSMSL.It was included in the International Polar
Year project 'Long-term Sea Level Variability in the Nordic Seas (LEVANS)'
which ran from 2007-2009 and was funded by the Research Council of Norway. 
The Project Team included Vladimir Pavlov (Project co-ordinator) from the
Norwegian Polar Institute and Igor Ashik from the Arctic and Antarctic Research
Institute. The data spans the period 1952-1990.We probably will not get any
futher
data from this station.
