=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 24-Jan-2011 :
Formentera is a new station data from 2009 and forms part of the newly established REDMAR network.
Gauge is a MIROS radar sensor. Benchmark NGAB MAR.FORMENT. 1.648m relative to the
REDMAR datum and 1.408m relative to IGN. See paper B. Perez et al 'Overlapping sea
level time series measured using different technologies: an example from the REDMAR Spanish network'.
