=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 17-OCT-05 :
According to John Hannah, the data at Dunedin is of very poor quality
with many small inconsistencies in the tide gauge zero.
Subsidence is occuring in the whole wharf area. Subsidence at the gauge
is now 1mm/yr.
