=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 24-Jan-2011 :
La Palma is on the island of La Palma in the Canaries. It is a new station first
data 2009 it forms part of the newly established REDMAR network. Gauge is a MIROS
radar sensor. Benchmark Pto.6.020 is 4.302m relative to the REDMAR datum.
See paper B. Perez et al 'Overlapping sea leveltime series measured using
different technologies: an example from the REDMAR Spanish network'.
