=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 24-Jan-2011 :
Sagunto is a new station first data 2007 it forms part of the newly established REDMAR network.
Gauge is a MIROS radar sensor. Benchmark MAREO-SAGUNTO is 3.875m relative to REDMAR
datum and 3.959m relative to IGN. Station made RLR. RLR year 2008 factor 7.025m.
Following intial instalment hardware problems (de-lamination) were experienced
supplied data has been adjusted accordingly. See paper by B. Perez et al 'Overlapping sea
level time series measured using different technologies: an example from the REDMAR Spanish network'.
