=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 20-Oct-2011 :
Using information from the National Tidal Centre, Australia Port Lincoln has been made RLR from May 1965 onwards. Data prior to this was not made RLR as it is not included on the National Tidal Centre website. The benchmark does not have a name/number but is described as 'Bolt in concrete at base of flagpole at Harbor Master's office'. Datum is chart datum 4.610m below TGBM. RLR factor is 6.090m.
