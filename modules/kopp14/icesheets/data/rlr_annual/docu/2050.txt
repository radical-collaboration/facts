=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 24-Jan-2011 :
Granadilla is a new station on the island of Tenerife first data 2003. Gauge
is a MIROS radar sensor.Data has previously been supplied but latitude and
longitude only now confirmed. Benchmark AP1 3.850m relative to
datum REDMAR .Station made RLR. RLR year is 2004 factor 5.450m.
See paper B. Perez et al 'Overlapping sea level time series measured using
different technologies: an example from the REDMAR Spanish network'.
