=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 13-Jul-2010 :   
Teriberka is a new station to PSMSL. It was include in the International Polar
Year project 'Long-term Sea Level Variability in the Nordic seas. The project
ran from 2007-2209 and was funded by the Research Council of Norway.
The Project team included Vladimir Pavlov (Project co-ordination) from the
Norwegian Polar Institute and Igor Ashik from the Arctic and Antarctic Research
Institute.
The data spans 1941-1990. It is unlikely we will receive any further data from
this station.
